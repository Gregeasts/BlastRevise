BlastRevise

About BlastRevise

BlastRevise is an AI-powered quiz app designed to generate topic-based questions at different difficulty levels. Choose your topic, select a difficulty, and get quizzed instantly!

How It Works

  • Enter a topic and the number of questions you want.
  • Select a question format and difficulty level.
  • Click "Generate Quiz" to get your quiz.
  • Answer the questions and submit to check results.
  • Use "Reset Questions Memory" to clear previously generated questions.
